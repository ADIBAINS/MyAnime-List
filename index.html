My list

Frieren: A fantasy series about Frieren, an elf mage, who reflects on her long life after the defeat of the Demon King. It explores themes of time, memory, and human connection.

Attack on Titan: Humanity fights for survival against massive, flesh-eating Titans. Eren Yeager leads the charge, uncovering dark secrets about their world in this intense, action-packed series with complex themes.

Bleach: Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager with the ability to see spirits, becomes a Soul Reaper tasked with protecting the living and battling malevolent spirits known as Hollows in a thrilling supernatural adventure.

Monster: Dr. Kenzo Tenma's life spirals after saving a young boy who grows up to become a psychopathic killer. A psychological thriller exploring morality, identity, and the nature of evil.

Your Name: Two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, mysteriously swap bodies and form a deep connection. A beautiful tale about love, time, and fate set against a backdrop of breathtaking animation.

Hajime No Ippo: The story of Ippo Makunouchi, a shy high schooler who becomes a professional boxer. His journey is filled with intense training, heart-pounding matches, and personal growth in a competitive world.

Jujutsu Kaisen: Yuji Itadori, a high schooler with immense physical strength, battles cursed spirits after ingesting a powerful cursed object. The anime blends supernatural action with humor and emotional depth.

Demon Slayer: Tanjiro Kamado becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his sister is turned into one. A visually stunning story of determination, family, and courage.

Dandadan: A bizarre mix of supernatural battles and absurd humor, this series follows Momo and Ken as they encounter aliens and spirits. It blends fast-paced action with quirky, off-the-wall moments.

Doraemon: A futuristic robot cat from the 22nd century, Doraemon helps a young boy, Nobita, navigate everyday life with gadgets from the future. A beloved series filled with heartwarming lessons and comedy.

Pokemon: Ash Ketchum and his trusty Pikachu journey across regions to become the ultimate Pokémon Master. This iconic series blends adventure, friendship, and battles in a colorful, ever-expanding universe.

Shinchan: A hilarious slice-of-life comedy centered around Shin-chan, a mischievous 5-year-old boy whose antics and unpredictable behavior drive his family and friends crazy in humorous, often absurd scenarios.

Berserk: Guts, a lone mercenary, is drawn into a dark, brutal world of demons, betrayal, and power struggles. A deeply philosophical tale of vengeance, camaraderie, and the cost of ambition.

Devilman Crybaby: Ryo Asuka, a mysterious figure, transforms his friend Akira Fudo into Devilman, a powerful being caught between humanity and demons. A tragic, violent exploration of human nature and apocalyptic chaos.

Chainsaw Man: Denji, a broke young man, becomes a devil hunter after merging with his pet devil, Pochita. This dark comedy-action series is known for its brutal violence, humor, and unpredictable twists.

Idaten Jump: A group of kids forms a mountain bike team and competes in extreme races while uncovering secrets and facing tough rivals. The series is fast-paced and filled with adventurous, action-packed moments.

Kaiju No.8: Kafka Hibino, a sanitation worker, gains the ability to transform into a giant monster. This action-packed series explores his quest to protect humanity while grappling with his new monstrous identity.

Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama is an animated film that retells the epic Hindu story of Prince Rama's quest to rescue his wife, Sita, from the demon king Ravana, highlighting virtues of honor, loyalty, and heroism.

Death Note: A high school student, Light Yagami, gains a notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. A high-stakes cat-and-mouse battle with the detective L ensues, exploring morality.

Neon Genesis Evangelion is a psychological mecha anime blending giant robots, existential dread, and deep character trauma. Shinji battles monstrous Angels while confronting identity, loneliness, and humanity’s fate in a surreal apocalypse.
